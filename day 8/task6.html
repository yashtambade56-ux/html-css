TASK 6: Grid Item – Column Span

Q1. What are grid lines?
→ Grid lines are the vertical and horizontal lines that divide the grid.
Q2. How many columns does the item span?
→ The item spans 2 columns.
Q3. What happens to other items?
→ Other items move to the next available space.
Q4. Can two items overlap?
→ No, unless forced using positioning.
Q5. Where is column spanning used in websites?
→ In headers, banners, and featured sections.
